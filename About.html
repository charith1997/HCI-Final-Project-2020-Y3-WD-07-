ABOUT US




WELCOME TO SAMARASINGHEOPT.COM

Humble Beginnings

A. A. Samarasinghe Optometrists founder, late Mr. A. A. Samarasinghe started his career in 1943 as a trainee optometrist under D. L.F. Prediris of William Perdiris & Company in Pettah. Consequently in 1968, he opened his own store in Kandy. Following great success in Kandy, Mr. A. A. Samarasinghe created the name as a well-respected and reputed gentlemen with hard work, focusing primarily on delivering the best customer service.

After his era, his sons continued in his aspiring work and have dedicated themselves to keep up his moral practices by helping communities and customers to have a better vision while reaching the pinnacle of success.. Presently there are 15 well established outlets in Sri Lanka.

OUR VISION

We take care your vision as the leader in South Asia

OUR MISSION

As a centre of excellence in the eye care service in Sri Lanka, this institute provides adequate quality services to satisfy patients through maximum tertiary level eye care with advance technology and with kindness of well trained staff and well maintain quality. It also gives necessary training and guidance for all categories of staff in the eye care service in Sri Lanka. We Support and conduct community eye care programs also in Sri Lanka.

OUR GOALS

1. To become best recognized centre in tertiary eye care in South Asia within next five years.
2. To develop uptodate training programs for each category of health staff suitable for local and international level in eye care.
3. To establish an international research centre and,
4. To sustain as a centre of excellence in eye care in Sri Lanka.
5. To sustain equality for service recipient regardless their income or status.
